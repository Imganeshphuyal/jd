About OSIS International

Founded in Kathmandu, OSIS International has established itself as a leading provider of geophysical and engineering consulting services across Nepal. Our team of experts combines decades of experience with cutting-edge technology to deliver innovative solutions tailored to Nepal's unique geological challenges.

We serve clients across multiple sectors including infrastructure development, hydropower, mining, construction, environmental management, and government agencies. Our commitment to excellence and client satisfaction has made us the preferred partner for complex projects throughout Nepal.

While we currently focus on serving Nepal's growing development needs, we're actively preparing for international expansion to bring our specialized expertise to global markets.

At OSIS International, we believe in sustainable development and responsible resource management, ensuring our solutions benefit both our clients and Nepal's precious environment.
